Making plot points

Portent Generator

From The Madey Upey Namey Emulator
The portent generator gives a list of random words which you can use as a basis for certain events. For instance, lets say you ask an NPC, "What quests are there around?" and the Portent Generator gives you "examination" and "slice." Here, you could interpret as they need a surgeon or medical proffessional to perform surgery to figure out what's been going on in the town. So, you undertake the task, and what you find: "coal" and "atmosphere" according to the Portent Generator. Alright, so you could interpret that as the town's local coal mining has been polluting the local atmosphere and giving people horrible lung issues. This is just one use of this system, and its only limited by your imagination! Of course, there are some prompts that might just stump you of how to integrate, and if that happens, thats ok, just roll again! Of course, always try to make some sense of it, but if what you find after doing surgery is a conversation, well, roll again!

How to use:
Simply type in the amount of words you'd like the Portent Generator to generate (I'd suggest 2,) and press generate! Simple as simple.

Oracle

From The Madey Upey Namey Emulator
Adding onto the portent generator is the Oracle, which, when you are asking yes or no questions, can answer them for you, and even add onto them!

Simply press generate, and it'll output its response.

2 No.
3 No, but...
4 Yes, but...
