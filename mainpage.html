Welcome to Into the Wilds!

This is a website designed to help with travel and solo play within Dungeons & Dragons, from providing a simple framework and plenty of ideas you can hop off from, to a full simulator of the systems provided here! With these tools to assist you, you can run travel that will be fun and engaging to your players, and if you alone are your players, our tools can help assist with how to run Combat, Exploration, and even Roleplay alone, as long as you have a flexible brain!

The style of play we provide with Into the Wilds is designed for slower, lighthearted, role-play centered playstyles, where the point is to stop and enjoy the scenery (at least the scenery in your imagination!) This site can be used intermittently throughout an adventure, it does not need to be present throughout all of it; if you have something else in mind, go ahead and do that instead!

This site uses a host of rules compiled from other official and non-official sources, from the Player's Handbook, the Dungeon Master's Guide, Ghosts of Saltmarsh, the Madey Upey Namey Emulator.
